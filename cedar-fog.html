About the Project

Cedar & Fog is an upgraded cafe website experience built around slow evenings, rain against old windows, warm coffee, and soft amber light.

The newer direction expands the site into a fuller digital world: a cinematic hero, responsive menu system, interactive rainy-day playlist, editorial journal section, and a warm visit flow that keeps the atmosphere consistent from first scroll to final detail.

Cinematic Cafe World

Rain, forest imagery, warm lighting, and layered contrast make the site feel like a place visitors can step into.

Menu & Playlist Flow

Food, drink, and music are designed as one sensory path, from misty menu cards to the house playlist.

Editorial Visit Journey

Journal content, local lore, and visit details give the page a fuller story while keeping the next step clear.
