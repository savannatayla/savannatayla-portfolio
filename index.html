ABOUT

Hi, I’m Savanna.

I’m a creative web designer and digital creative with a passion for building immersive, visually-driven websites that feel memorable, emotional, and uniquely tailored to each brand.

I specialize in modern portfolio sites, business websites, and aesthetic digital experiences that blend storytelling with functionality. My style leans toward cinematic visuals, thoughtful interactions, and clean user-focused design.

With experience in design, branding, customer communication, and creative problem-solving, I love helping ideas transform into polished online spaces that feel alive and intentional.

A lot of my inspiration comes from Pinterest, TikTok, films, television, books, and atmospheric storytelling. I’m drawn to visuals that evoke emotion and create a distinct feeling, and I bring that same energy into every project I design.

When I’m not designing, you’ll probably find me reading romance novels, working on creative projects, spending time with my cats Eli and Winsie, or finding inspiration in art, games, and aesthetic media.
