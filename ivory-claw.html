About the Project

Ivory Claw is a luxury nail atelier that needed a digital presence as refined as the work itself. The direction was editorial, tactile, and easy to book from.

The page uses soft contrast, polished imagery, confident serif type, and a calm booking rhythm so the site feels like a luxury appointment before the visitor even arrives.

Editorial First Impression

Large visuals and elegant spacing give the brand an immediate campaign-like quality.

Smoother Booking Flow

Services, style direction, and inquiry cues are presented in a way that feels premium instead of clinical.

Luxury Details

Subtle motion, warm neutrals, and focused calls to action keep the experience polished.
