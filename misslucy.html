About the Project

Miss Lucy is a bright ESL website concept for a South African English teacher offering online lessons for children and adults.

The design balances playful learning energy with a practical service structure: clear program paths, simple package options, learner reviews, and repeated trial lesson prompts so visitors always know the next step.

Clear Learning Paths

Kids and adult programs are split into simple cards so different audiences can quickly find the right lesson style.

Package-Ready Flow

The pricing section is prepared for payment links and keeps trial lessons, weekly lessons, and focused support easy to compare.

Trust-Led Details

Friendly visuals, review cards, and confident calls to action help the site feel warm, credible, and ready for real learners.
